About

This edition was created by Cassidy Holahan, a Ph.D. canidate in the English Department at the University of Pennsylvania specializing in British literature of the long eighteenth century. More about Cassidy and her work can be found at her website, www.cassidyholahan.com. All inquiries about this edition can be sent to cholahan@sas.upenn.edu

This edition was created using Manicule, a software designed and built by Whitney Trettien and Liza Daly. More information on the software can be found below.

This project would not have been possible without Whitney Trettien, who assisted and advised at every stage. Special thanks to Arthur Mitchell Fraas and John Pollack at the Kislak Center for assisting in the digitization process.
